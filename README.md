# SCT_DA_4 - Business Insights Report (EDA)

## Objective

Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a marketing campaign dataset to identify customer behavior, purchasing patterns, marketing funnel performance, and purchase channel trends.

The analysis aims to generate business insights and provide data-driven marketing budget recommendations.

## Dataset

Marketing Campaign Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, income, product spending, purchase channels, web activity, and marketing campaign response data.

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Data Cleaning

- Identified and handled missing Income values
- Checked and removed duplicate records
- Converted customer date data to datetime format
- Created an Age feature
- Removed unrealistic age outliers
- Created Total Spending and Total Purchases features

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The following analyses were performed:

- Customer Age Distribution
- Customer Income Distribution
- Product Spending Analysis
- Purchase Channel Performance
- Marketing Campaign Funnel
- Campaign Response Rate
- Income vs Total Spending Analysis
- Customer Value Segmentation

## Key Business Insights

- The customer base is mainly concentrated among middle-aged customers.
- Higher-income customers generally demonstrate greater spending potential.
- Wine and meat products contribute significantly to customer spending.
- Purchase activity differs across Web, Catalog, and Store channels.
- The marketing campaign response rate is relatively low.
- High-value customers are important targets for personalized campaigns.

## Business Recommendation

The strongest purchase channel should receive greater marketing attention based on observed purchase performance.

High-value customers should be targeted through personalized offers, loyalty programs, and premium product promotions.

Marketing campaigns should focus on high-performing product categories and use customer segmentation to improve campaign conversion.

Since the dataset does not contain channel-level marketing costs, the recommendation is based on purchase performance and customer engagement rather than exact ROI.
